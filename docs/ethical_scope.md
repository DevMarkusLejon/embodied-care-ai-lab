# Ethical Scope

This project is for research and educational purposes only.

It does not provide medical advice, does not diagnose conditions, and does not replace clinical judgment. Users should seek qualified medical care for personal health concerns and emergency services for urgent symptoms.

The initial benchmark uses synthetic prompts only. Do not add private patient data, identifiable clinical records, or confidential health information to this repository.

The project deliberately avoids treating benchmark results as clinical validation. A model that scores well on these examples has not been proven safe for medical use. A keyword-based evaluator can miss harmful advice, reward superficial phrasing, and fail to capture clinical nuance.

Future clinical or user-facing work would require:

- clinical expert review;
- formal safety evaluation;
- privacy and security controls;
- ethics or IRB review where applicable;
- compliance with relevant laws and regulations;
- careful evaluation of model deployment context and failure modes.

The project should also avoid ideological overclaiming. Its technical focus is subgroup-aware evaluation, human-centered AI behavior, and care-centered system design.
